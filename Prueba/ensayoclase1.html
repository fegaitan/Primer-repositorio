DJ, producer and composer

During the last 10 years, Francisco Gaitán has become a household name in the Japanese Techpara and Parapara scenes, after publishing dozens of remixes, EPs and a full album. He has also played as a DJ on some on famous Japanese venues like Maharaja Roppongi, Differ Ariake (now closed) and Pasela Akiba, and in all the most important parapara events in Japan.

Experience in all styles of Dance music

Since 2011 Francisco Gaitán has produced not only Hyper Techno and Eurobeat music but also House, Trance, Techno and Hardcore with his alias MHX, with productions being supported by DJs like Paul Van Dyk.
